# Privacy Policy for MobX DevTools

**Last Updated: December 10, 2024**

## Overview

MobX DevTools is a browser extension designed to help developers debug MobX state in web applications. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**MobX DevTools does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What the extension accesses:

- **Page Content**: The extension reads MobX observable state and actions from web pages you visit. This data is only displayed locally in Chrome DevTools and is never sent to any external server.

- **Local Storage**: The extension stores your preferences (such as selected stores for filtering) in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

### What the extension does NOT do:

- Does not collect any personal information
- Does not track your browsing history
- Does not send any data to external servers
- Does not use analytics or tracking tools
- Does not access cookies or authentication data
- Does not store any data outside your browser

## Data Security

All data processed by MobX DevTools remains entirely on your local machine within Chrome DevTools. The extension operates completely offline after installation.

## Third-Party Services

MobX DevTools does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
